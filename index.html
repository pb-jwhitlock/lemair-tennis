Private Lessons

100% personalized coaching with undivided attention. The fastest path to real improvement. Ideal for players serious about leveling up.

  • Video analysis available & instant feedback

Semi-Private

Semi-Private Lessons

Share the court with one partner. Focused coaching at a fraction of the private cost — perfect for couples, friends, or parent & child.

  • 2 players per session (bring your partner)
  • Personalized feedback for each player
  • Live match play & focused drills
  • Great for siblings, spouses, or teammates
Clinic

Group Clinics

High-energy sessions for 3–7 players. More court time, more points, and a chance to build your tennis community at the most accessible price.

  • 3–7 players per clinic
  • Skill-matched groups at all levels
  • High-volume drilling & point play
  • Weekly recurring schedule
  • Junior clinics available (ages 6–17)
